About The Course

Welcome to the fascinating field of Artificial Intelligence. This course provides a broad introduction to the core concepts and techniques that enable machines to learn, reason, and act. We will explore the history of AI, understand foundational algorithms, and discuss the ethical implications of creating intelligent systems. Prepare to challenge your thinking and discover the potential of AI to solve real-world problems.

Key Topics:

  • Search Algorithms & Problem Solving
  • Introduction to Machine Learning
  • Neural Networks & Deep Learning
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP)
  • AI Ethics and Societal Impact
